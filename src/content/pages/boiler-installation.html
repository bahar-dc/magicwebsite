Types of Boilers We Install

We install all types of gas boilers to suit different property sizes and hot water demands. Here's a guide to help you understand your options.

  • Combi Boilers

    The most popular choice for smaller homes. Combi boilers provide heating and instant hot water without the need for a separate cylinder or tank. Ideal for flats and homes with 1-2 bathrooms.

  • System Boilers

    System boilers work with a hot water cylinder to store heated water. They're suitable for larger homes with multiple bathrooms where hot water demand is higher.

  • Conventional Boilers

    Also known as regular or heat-only boilers, these work with both a cylinder and a cold water tank. Often found in older properties with existing heating systems.

  • Boiler Conversions

    We can convert your existing system to a different boiler type if it better suits your needs, such as upgrading from a conventional boiler to a more efficient combi.
