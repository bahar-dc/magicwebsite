About Us

Our Story

GB Boilers was established by Greg Bradley, who learnt his trade back in Herefordshire, where he did an apprenticeship with a family member. This traditional foundation in craftsmanship and hands-on training has shaped everything we do.

For nearly 20 years, GB Boilers has prided itself on honesty, reliability and doing the best job for the customer. We believe that good work speaks for itself, and our reputation has been built through word-of-mouth recommendations from satisfied customers.

Today, we continue to serve homeowners and businesses across Esher, Claygate, Chessington and the surrounding Surrey areas with the same commitment to quality that has defined our business from the start.

Our Values

These are the principles that guide everything we do at GB Boilers:

  • Honesty

    We tell you what you need to know, not what we think you want to hear. If a repair will fix the problem, we won't recommend a replacement. If your boiler is beyond economical repair, we'll tell you straight.

  • Reliability

    When we say we'll be there, we'll be there. We understand that your time is valuable, and we respect that by turning up when we say we will and completing work when we say we will.

  • Quality

    We take pride in our work and don't cut corners. Every job, whether it's a simple service or a complete installation, receives the same attention to detail and commitment to doing things properly.

  • Safety

    As a Gas Safe registered engineer, safety is at the heart of everything we do. We follow all regulations and best practices to ensure your home and family are protected.
