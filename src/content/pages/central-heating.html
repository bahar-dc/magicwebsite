Heating Issues

Common Central Heating Problems

Cold Radiators: If one or more radiators aren't heating up properly, it could be air in the system, a stuck valve, or sludge buildup. We can diagnose and fix the issue quickly.

Noisy System: Banging, gurgling or whistling noises often indicate air in the system, low water pressure, or limescale buildup. These issues shouldn't be ignored as they can worsen over time.

Uneven Heating: If some rooms are warm while others stay cold, your system may need balancing, or there could be circulation problems that need addressing.

Leaking Radiators: Leaks can occur at valves, joints or from the radiator body itself. We can repair or replace leaking components to prevent water damage.

High Energy Bills: An inefficient heating system costs more to run. We can assess your system and recommend improvements to reduce your energy consumption.
