About GB Boilers

Your Trusted Local Plumber

GB Boilers was established by Greg Bradley, who learnt his trade back in Herefordshire through an apprenticeship with a family member. This traditional foundation in craftsmanship has shaped everything we do.

For nearly 20 years, GB Boilers has prided itself on honesty, reliability and doing the best job for the customer. We believe in straightforward communication, fair pricing and workmanship you can trust.

Based locally, we serve homeowners and businesses throughout Esher, Claygate, Chessington and the surrounding Surrey areas. Our reputation has been built on recommendations from satisfied customers who appreciate our professional, no-nonsense approach.
