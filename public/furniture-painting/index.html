Specialist Furniture Painting

FURNITURE PAINTING

Craftsmanship That Brings Your Furniture to Life

Furniture can completely transform a room when finished with care and creativity. At Magic Painting, we offer specialist furniture painting services that breathe new character into your beloved pieces, whether you're restoring a family heirloom or refreshing a modern feature.

Our Fulham based team understands that furniture deserves the same level of attention as any wall or ceiling. With a blend of traditional skill and contemporary finishes, we deliver elegant, long-lasting results that elevate your space.

Our Approach

Meticulous Techniques, Stunning Results

From hand painted wardrobes to flawless kitchen cabinets, our artisans use techniques developed through years of experience. We work with premium primers and paints designed specifically for wood and other surfaces, ensuring excellent adhesion and a smooth, durable finish.

Whether you're after a classic matte look, a high gloss statement, or something in between, we'll guide you through the best options for your space and style.

  • Transform your built-in wardrobes and cupboards with our bespoke painting service. We carefully prepare each surface to ensure perfect adhesion, and apply multiple coats of high-quality paint for a smooth, long-lasting finish that blends seamlessly with your interior design.

  • Refresh the heart of your home with our kitchen cabinet and island painting service. This is a cost-effective way to completely transform your kitchen without the expense of replacement. We use specialized primers and paints designed to withstand the unique conditions of kitchen environments—resistant to moisture, heat, and frequent cleaning.

  • Give new life to cherished furniture pieces with our custom painting service. Whether it's an antique dresser, dining table, chairs, or bookcase, we can transform any stand-alone furniture item to complement your décor. Our meticulous preparation and application techniques ensure beautiful results that will last for years to come.

  • Transform ordinary or tired furniture into extraordinary statement pieces through our creative upcycling service. We can help bring your vision to life with specialty finishes, decorative techniques, and creative approaches that turn mundane items into unique, personalized treasures that reflect your style and personality.

Why Magic Painting?

We bring the same precision and respect to furniture as we do to walls and buildings. Every piece is prepped, painted, and finished with the utmost care. Our team is tidy, punctual, and dedicated to protecting your property throughout the process.

If you're ready to transform your furniture into something special, Magic Painting is here to help.
