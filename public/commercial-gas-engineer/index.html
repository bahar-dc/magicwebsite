Commercial Gas Services

GB Boilers provides professional commercial gas engineering services for businesses, landlords and property managers across Esher, Claygate, Thames Ditton and the surrounding Surrey areas.

Commercial gas work requires specific qualifications and experience. As a Gas Safe registered engineer with the appropriate commercial certifications, Greg Bradley is qualified to work on commercial heating systems, issue landlord gas safety certificates, and ensure your business premises comply with all relevant regulations.

Whether you manage a single rental property or a portfolio of commercial buildings, we provide reliable, professional service with minimal disruption to your tenants or business operations.

  • Commercial Boiler Installation

For Landlords

Landlord Gas Safety Requirements

As a landlord, you have a legal duty to ensure all gas appliances, fittings and flues in your rental properties are safe. This includes arranging an annual gas safety check by a Gas Safe registered engineer.

Annual Inspections: Gas safety checks must be carried out every 12 months. We can set up reminders and schedule regular visits to ensure you never miss a deadline.

CP12 Certificates: After each inspection, we provide a Gas Safety Certificate (CP12) which you must give to your tenants within 28 days of the check, or to new tenants before they move in.

Record Keeping: You must keep copies of gas safety records for at least 2 years. We provide clear documentation and can help you maintain proper records.

High Energy Bills: An inefficient heating system costs more to run. We can assess your system and recommend improvements to reduce your energy consumption.

Multiple Properties: If you manage several rental properties, we can arrange efficient scheduling to service all your properties with minimal hassle.
