Our Services

Professional Boiler Servicing

Regular boiler servicing is essential for keeping your heating system running safely and efficiently. At GB Boilers, we provide thorough annual boiler services for homeowners across Esher, Claygate, Chessington and the surrounding Surrey areas.

An annual boiler service helps identify potential problems before they become expensive repairs, extends the lifespan of your boiler, and ensures your system is operating at peak efficiency. For landlords, regular servicing is a legal requirement to maintain a valid Gas Safety Certificate.

As a Gas Safe registered engineer with nearly 20 years of experience, Greg Bradley provides professional servicing for all major boiler brands including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, Ideal and more.

What's Included in a Boiler Service

During a comprehensive boiler service, we carry out a series of checks and tests to ensure your heating system is safe and working correctly.

  • Visual Inspection

    We check the boiler casing, pipework, flue and ventilation for any signs of damage, corrosion or deterioration.

  • Internal Components

    We inspect and clean key internal components including the burner, heat exchanger and main injector.

  • Gas Pressure & Flow

    We test gas pressure and flow rates to ensure the boiler is receiving the correct amount of gas for safe operation.

  • Flue Analysis

    We perform a flue gas analysis to check combustion efficiency and ensure emissions are within safe limits.

  • Safety Controls

    We test all safety devices and controls to ensure they are functioning correctly and will shut down the boiler if needed.

  • Service Report

    You receive a detailed service report and any recommendations for repairs or improvements to your system.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Regular Boiler Servicing

Safety First: A boiler service checks for gas leaks, carbon monoxide risks and other potential hazards. Your family's safety is the most important reason to service your boiler annually.

Lower Energy Bills: A well-maintained boiler operates more efficiently, using less gas to heat your home. Regular servicing can help reduce your energy costs.

Prevent Breakdowns: Servicing identifies worn parts and potential issues before they cause a complete breakdown, often at the worst possible time.

Extend Boiler Life: Just like a car, regular maintenance helps your boiler last longer. A well-serviced boiler can operate efficiently for 15 years or more.

Maintain Warranty: Most boiler manufacturers require annual servicing by a Gas Safe registered engineer to keep your warranty valid.
